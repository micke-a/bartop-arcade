# 24" Ply 2 Player Bartop Arcade Cabinet Flat Pack Kit

High Quality, CNC produced Cabinet

## Overall Dimensions:

* Height :  67cm
* Width  : 60cm (56.4cm internal)
* Depth  : 50cm

## Comes with:

IEC Switched Socket

Keku Clips & Screws to make Control Panel Removable

Door Lock & 2 keys

All Dowels and Cams to assemble the unit.

2 angle brackets for monitor mount bar.

(allows for variable monitor depth)

Holes cut for 28mm Diameter Buttons.

Control Panel at 5 degree angle.

Rear rebated to assist joystick alignment.

## Design Notes:

Intention is for monitor to be 'framed' by vinyl gfx attached to acrylic monitor cover & acrylic marquee to have vinyl gfx applied and be lit from the rear.

Cabinet can be stained, coated or painted easily.

Can accept 2-way speakers (message to confirm sizing)

Monitor should be fitted 'up to' the acrylic monitor cover using the variable mounting brackets

Accepts flat screen monitor Size, up to 56.4cm wide x 35.5cm high
(i.e. most 24" widescreen)

**ACRYLIC NOT SUPPLIED**

Required Acrylic Sizes Are:

* Screen: 570 x 385 x 2mm
* Marquee: 570 x 117 x 2mm
